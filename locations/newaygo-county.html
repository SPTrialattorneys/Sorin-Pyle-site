Newaygo County Criminal Defense Lawyer

When you're facing criminal charges in Newaygo County, you need a defense attorney who is familiar with the local legal landscape. Sorin & Pyle provides dedicated representation for clients in White Cloud, Fremont, Newaygo, and the surrounding areas.

Your Advocate in Newaygo County Courts

Our attorneys have the experience necessary to effectively navigate the Newaygo County court system, including:

  • 27th Circuit Court: This court has jurisdiction over all felony cases in Newaygo County. Our trial lawyers are prepared to defend your most serious charges here.
  • 78th District Court: Located in White Cloud, this court handles all misdemeanor offenses and the preliminary stages of felony cases. We are experienced in representing clients at every stage in this court.

Don't face the Newaygo County justice system alone. Our firm is committed to providing a robust defense for every client. Contact us today for a free consultation to learn how we can protect your rights.
