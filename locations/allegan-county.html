Allegan County Criminal Defense Lawyer

If you're facing criminal charges in Allegan County, having a local attorney who knows the system is crucial. Sorin & Pyle has a strong track record of defending clients in the Allegan County courts against a wide range of misdemeanor and felony charges.

Experienced Defense in Allegan County Courts

Our firm is intimately familiar with the courts and legal personnel of Allegan County. We regularly handle cases in:

  • 48th Circuit Court: This is the trial court for all felony cases in Allegan County. Our experienced trial lawyers are prepared to defend your rights and your future in front of a jury at the main courthouse in the city of Allegan.
  • 57th District Court: This court, also located in Allegan, handles the initial stages of all felony cases (like arraignments and preliminary examinations) and all misdemeanor cases from start to finish. Whether you're charged with OWI, domestic violence, or another offense, your case will begin here.

We understand the local procedures and have built professional relationships within the Allegan County legal community. This local knowledge can be a significant advantage in your defense. Whether you were arrested in Allegan, Plainwell, Otsego, or Saugatuck, we are ready to fight for you. Contact us for a free consultation to discuss your Allegan County case.
