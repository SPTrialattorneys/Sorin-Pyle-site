Kalamazoo County Criminal Defense Lawyer

Facing criminal charges in Kalamazoo County requires a defense attorney with local insight. Sorin & Pyle provides skilled representation for clients in Kalamazoo, Portage, and the surrounding areas, fighting both felony and misdemeanor charges.

Defending Clients in Kalamazoo County Courts

Our attorneys are well-versed in the procedures and personnel of the Kalamazoo County court system. We regularly appear in:

  • 9th Circuit Court: As the court for all felony cases in the county, we are prepared to take your case to trial here, defending your freedom and future.
  • 8th District Court: With locations in both Kalamazoo and Portage, this court handles all misdemeanor cases and the critical early stages of felony cases. We have extensive experience navigating both divisions of this court.

A local charge needs a local defense. We understand the nuances of practicing in Kalamazoo County and use that knowledge to your advantage. Contact us for a free consultation to see how we can help with your case.
