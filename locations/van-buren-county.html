Van Buren County Criminal Defense Lawyer

If you are facing criminal charges in Van Buren County, the experienced attorneys at Sorin & Pyle are here to help. We provide skilled legal defense for clients in Paw Paw, South Haven, and across the county.

Navigating the Van Buren County Court System

Our firm is experienced in handling cases in the courts that serve Van Buren County. We regularly represent clients in:

  • 36th Circuit Court: This court, located in Paw Paw, has jurisdiction over all felony cases in the county. Our trial lawyers are prepared to defend you against the most serious charges.
  • 7th District Court: With locations in both Paw Paw and South Haven, this court handles all misdemeanor cases and the initial stages of felony matters. We are familiar with the processes in both court locations.

No matter the charge, we are ready to fight for you. We understand the local legal landscape and are committed to securing the best possible outcome for our clients in Van Buren County. Contact us for a free consultation.
