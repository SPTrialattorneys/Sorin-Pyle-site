Ottawa County Criminal Defense Lawyer

From our home office in Holland, we are proud to be one of Ottawa County's premier criminal defense firms. If you've been charged with a crime in Holland, Grand Haven, Hudsonville, or anywhere in the county, you need a local team that knows the system inside and out.

Deep Experience in Ottawa County Courts

As local attorneys, we have a deep and personal understanding of the Ottawa County justice system. We regularly defend our neighbors in:

  • 20th Circuit Court: Located in Grand Haven, this is the court where all felony cases are tried. Our lawyers have a strong presence and a respected reputation in this court.
  • 58th District Court: This court serves the entire county with three locations in Grand Haven, Holland, and Hudsonville. We have extensive experience handling misdemeanor and felony preliminary matters in all three courthouses and are familiar with their unique procedures.

This is our home turf. We have a proven track record of success defending clients against a wide range of criminal charges in Ottawa County. Contact us for a free consultation and put our local advantage to work for you.
