Our Practice Areas

We are equipped to handle any felony or misdemeanor charge in Michigan. If you don't see your specific charge listed, we can still help. Your fight is our fight.

Felonies & High Court Misdemeanors

A felony is a serious crime punishable by more than one year in prison. High court misdemeanors carry up to two years and are treated like felonies for sentencing and many other purposes.

  • Murder
  • Robbery
  • Home Invasion
  • Felony OWI
  • Embezzlement
  • Major Drug Crimes
  • Felonious Assault
  • Carrying a Concealed Weapon (CCW)
  • Resisting & Obstructing a Police Officer (R&O)

Misdemeanors

A misdemeanor is a less serious offense, typically punishable by up to one year in county jail and fines.

  • OWI / Drunk Driving
  • Domestic Violence
  • Assault and Battery
  • Larceny / Theft
  • Retail Fraud (Shoplifting)
  • Malicious Destruction of Property (MDOP)
  • Driving While License Suspended (DWLS)
  • Minor in Posession of Alcohol (MIP)
  • Misdemeanor Traffic Violations

Post-Conviction & Administrative Law

Expungements

A past mistake shouldn't define your future. We can help clear your record through the expungement process, opening doors to new opportunities.

Driver License Restoration

We represent clients at DLAD/DAAD hearings to help them restore their driving privileges after a suspension or revocation.

Appeals

If you received an unjust outcome at trial, you have the right to appeal. We meticulously review trial records to fight for a better result.

Juvenile Defense

We protect the rights and futures of young people facing charges in the juvenile justice system, ensuring they receive a fair process.
