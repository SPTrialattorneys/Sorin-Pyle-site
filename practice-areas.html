Our Practice Areas

If you are facing a criminal charge, we can help. While this page lists our most common case types, we are equipped to handle any felony or misdemeanor charge in Michigan. Your fight is our fight.

Felony & Misdemeanor Charges

OWI/Drunk Driving

Facing a drunk driving charge can be overwhelming. We meticulously examine every detail, from the traffic stop to the chemical test.

Domestic Violence

These charges are incredibly sensitive. We handle domestic violence cases with the discretion and tenacity they require.

All Drug Related Charges

From simple possession to complex trafficking charges, we provide a vigorous defense to protect your future and your freedom.

Murder

We provide a robust defense for individuals facing the most serious of charges, ensuring every legal avenue is explored.

Criminal Sexual Conduct (CSC)

Accusations of CSC carry life-altering consequences. We handle these sensitive cases with discretion and an aggressive defense strategy.

Robbery

Whether armed or unarmed, robbery charges are severe. We are prepared to challenge the prosecution's case at every turn.

Assault and Battery

We defend your rights and your story when you're accused of assault, fighting to achieve the best possible outcome.

Home Invasion

A conviction for home invasion can lead to a long prison sentence. We build a strong defense to protect your liberty.

Arson

Arson investigations are complex. We have the experience to challenge forensic evidence and witness testimony.

Larceny

We handle all types of larceny cases, from petty theft to grand larceny, with a focus on protecting your record.

Embezzlement

Accusations of embezzlement can ruin careers. We provide a sophisticated defense for these complex financial cases.

Retail Fraud

Often called shoplifting, retail fraud charges can have lasting consequences. We work to minimize the impact on your life.

Malicious Destruction of Property

We defend clients against MDOP charges, working to mitigate penalties and achieve a favorable resolution.

Driving While License Suspended

DWLS can lead to further suspensions and even jail time. We help you navigate the system and get back on the road legally.

Juvenile Charges

We protect the rights and futures of young people facing charges in the juvenile justice system.

Post-Conviction & Administrative Law

Expungements

A past mistake shouldn't define your future. We can help clear your record through the expungement process, opening doors to new opportunities.

Driver License Restoration

We represent clients at DLAD/DAAD hearings to help them restore their driving privileges after a suspension or revocation.

Appeals

If you received an unjust outcome at trial, you have the right to appeal. We meticulously review trial records to fight for a better result.
