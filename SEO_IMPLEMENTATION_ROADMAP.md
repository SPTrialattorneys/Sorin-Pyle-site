- Fix: Complete GBP optimization in Week 1

- Mistake: Copy-pasting same content with city name changes
- Why it's bad: Google penalizes duplicate content
- Fix: Write unique 500+ word content for each city

‚ùå **Inconsistent NAP across citations**
- Mistake: Using "Suite" in one citation, "Ste" in another
- Why it's bad: Confuses Google, hurts local rankings
- Fix: Use EXACT same format everywhere: "217 E 24th St Ste 107, Holland, MI 49423"

‚ùå **Keyword stuffing**
- Mistake: "DUI lawyer Holland MI DUI attorney Holland Michigan DUI defense lawyer"
- Why it's bad: Sounds unnatural, Google penalty
- Fix: Use keywords naturally, 1-2% density maximum

‚ùå **Ignoring mobile experience**
- Mistake: Only testing on desktop
- Why it's bad: Most criminal defense searches happen on mobile
- Fix: Test every page on mobile, ensure click-to-call works

‚ùå **Not responding to reviews**
- Mistake: Collecting reviews but never responding
- Why it's bad: Shows lack of engagement, hurts rankings
- Fix: Respond to ALL reviews within 24-48 hours

‚ùå **Creating pages without proper schema markup**
- Mistake: Adding service pages without schema
- Why it's bad: Misses rich results and enhanced search appearance
- Fix: Add Service schema to DUI and DV pages, LocalBusiness to city pages

‚ùå **Giving up too early**
- Mistake: Implementing for 4-6 weeks, seeing no results, stopping
- Why it's bad: SEO takes 3-6 months to show full results
