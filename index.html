Specialized Legal Services

Pre-Charge Investigations

When you're under investigation but haven't been charged yet, we step in early to protect your rights. Our proactive approach can prevent charges from being filed or significantly reduce their severity before you ever see a courtroom.

Record Expungement

Clear your criminal record and reclaim your future. We navigate Michigan's expungement process to help you seal past convictions, opening doors to better employment, housing, and educational opportunities.

Driver License Restoration

Lost your license due to DUI or other violations? We guide you through Michigan's DLAD/DAAD hearing process to restore your driving privileges and get you back on the road legally.

Personal Protection Orders (PPO)

Whether you need to obtain a PPO for your safety or defend against an unfair PPO petition, we provide skilled representation in Michigan's personal protection order proceedings. We understand the sensitive nature of these cases and work to protect your rights and safety.

Juvenile Defense

Young people deserve a second chance. We provide compassionate, aggressive defense for minors facing criminal charges, always keeping their future opportunities and educational goals as our top priority.
