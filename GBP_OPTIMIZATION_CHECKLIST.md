## üö® Common GBP Mistakes to Avoid

### Mistake #1: Keyword Stuffing in Business Name
**DON'T:** "Sorin & Pyle Criminal Defense Attorneys DUI Lawyers Holland MI"
**DO:** "Sorin & Pyle, PC"

**Why:** Google suspends profiles for keyword stuffing in business name. It's against guidelines and you'll lose your profile.

---

### Mistake #2: Using PO Box or Virtual Office
**DON'T:** List PO Box or virtual office as address
**DO:** List actual physical office where you meet clients

**Why:** Google requires street address where business operates. PO boxes get flagged.

---

### Mistake #3: Inconsistent NAP Across Web
**DON'T:** Different address formats on website vs GBP vs directories
**DO:** Use EXACT same format everywhere

**Why:** Inconsistency confuses Google and hurts local rankings significantly.

---

### Mistake #4: Ignoring Reviews
**DON'T:** Never respond to reviews
**DO:** Respond to ALL reviews within 24-48 hours

**Why:** Response rate is a ranking factor. Shows engagement.

---

### Mistake #5: One-Time Setup, Then Neglect
**DON'T:** Set up profile once, never update
**DO:** Post weekly, add photos monthly, continuously manage

**Why:** Freshness is a ranking signal. Active profiles outrank stale ones.

---

### Mistake #6: Fake Reviews or Review Gating
**DON'T:**
- Write fake reviews
- Ask friends/family to review
- Only ask satisfied clients (review gating)
- Pay for reviews

**DO:**
- Only request from actual clients
- Ask ALL clients (let them decide whether to review)
- Never incentivize reviews

**Why:** Google detects fake reviews and suspends profiles. Review gating violates Google policy.

---

### Mistake #7: Wrong Primary Category
**DON'T:** Set primary as "Lawyer" or "Attorney"
**DO:** Set primary as "Criminal Defense Attorney"

**Why:** Primary category determines what searches you appear in. Specific beats generic.

---

### Mistake #8: Posting Too Infrequently
**DON'T:** Post once a month or less
**DO:** Post weekly (minimum)

**Why:** Regular posting signals active business to Google.
