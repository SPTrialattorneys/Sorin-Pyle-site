üèõÔ∏è Michigan DV Law Specifics

‚ñº

What qualifies as domestic violence in Michigan:

  • Any assault/battery against spouse, ex-spouse, dating partner
  • Someone you have a child with
  • Current or former household member
  • NO physical contact required - threats count

Penalties:

  • First Offense: Up to 93 days jail + $500 fine
  • Second Offense: Up to 1 year jail + $1,000 fine
  • Third+ Offense: FELONY - Up to 5 years prison
  • Aggravated (injury): FELONY - Up to 10 years

Mandatory consequences: Probation, batterer's intervention program, no firearms.
