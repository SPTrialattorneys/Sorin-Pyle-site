### 3. MRPC 7.4 - Communication of Fields of Practice ✅

**Rule:** Prohibits stating or implying specialization unless certified by State Bar of Michigan or ABA-approved organization.

**Review Findings:**

**Violations Identified and CORRECTED (November 20, 2025):**

6 instances of prohibited "specializing" language found and replaced:

1. **attorneys.html (line 19)** - OG meta tag
   - Before: "specializing in DUI, felonies, and misdemeanors"
   - After: "defending DUI, felonies, and misdemeanors"

2. **record-expungement.html (line 105)** - Body copy
   - Before: "We specialize in petition-based expungement"
   - After: "We focus on petition-based expungement"

3. **card.html (line 49)** - Schema description
   - Before: "specializing in second chances"
   - After: "focusing on second chances"

4. **allendale-mi.html (line 18)** - OG meta tag
   - Before: "specializing in GVSU student defense"
   - After: "experienced in GVSU student defense"

5. **cdl-owi-defense.html (line 244)** - Body copy
   - Before: "attorney who specializes in CDL defense"
   - After: "attorney experienced in CDL defense"

6. **drivers-license-restoration.html (line 218)** - Bullet point
   - Before: "Specialized Knowledge"
   - After: "In-Depth Knowledge"

**Compliant Alternatives Used:**
- "defending" (stronger, action-oriented)
- "experienced in" (factual credential statement)
- "focusing on" (practice description)
- "In-Depth Knowledge" (descriptive, not certification claim)

**Remaining Uses of "Specialized" (All Compliant):**
- Descriptive only: "specialized defense" (describes type of defense, not attorney certification)
- Factual statements: "specialized area of law" (statement about law, not attorney credentials)
- References to others: Describing other attorneys' practices (not claims about firm)

**Status:** COMPLIANT - All violations corrected

## Appendix: Michigan Rules of Professional Conduct References

### MRPC 7.1 - Communications Concerning a Lawyer's Services
- Prohibits false or misleading statements
- Requires factual accuracy in all advertising
- Prohibits guarantees of results

### MRPC 7.2 - Advertising
- Governs content of attorney advertising
- Requires disclaimers where appropriate
- Regulates fee advertising and "free" claims

### MRPC 7.3 - Direct Contact with Prospective Clients
- Governs direct solicitation (not applicable to websites)

### MRPC 7.4 - Communication of Fields of Practice and Specialization
- **KEY RULE:** Cannot state or imply specialization unless certified
- Michigan has NO criminal law specialization certification program
- Must use alternatives: "experienced in," "focusing on," "practicing in"

### MRPC 7.5 - Firm Names and Letterheads
- Prohibits misleading firm names
- Regulates trade names and geographic designations
